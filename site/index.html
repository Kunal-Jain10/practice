Pasta

pasta is an Italian type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

Pizza

pizza is of Italian origin, consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients.

Noodles

Noodles are a type of food made from unleavened dough which is rolled flat and cut, stretched or extruded, into long strips or strings.
